About Us

When this start-up was first founded, we
used to spend our expenses without a
budget. All of them were used to purchase
the raw materials needed. This led to a
series of issues. For instance, we could
not afford fees for packaging, advertisements,
branding taxes and so on. We could not do
but to stop paying our employees their
salaries in order to prevent the company
from bankruptcy. Unfortunately, most of
them left. However, we were lucky to have
six of us who continued tostay working
without getting paid willingly. We had to
work ourselves in processes such as
fabrication, packaging and sales. The works
were tiring yet we were sincerely grateful
to have such strong partnership.

We are dedicated to sell masks with high quality to everyone.

We guarantee our product's quality while guaranteeing the quantity
